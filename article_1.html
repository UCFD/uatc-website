Rev. Jong Hyuk Kim’s Powerful Message to Students

February 19, 2025

During the recent visit of the General Assembly leadership to the university, Rev. Jong Hyuk Kim, Moderator of the General Assembly of the Presbyterian Church in Korea (Hapdong), delivered a powerful sermon to students and faculty members.

Preaching from Acts 17:10-15 on the theme “The Bible and the Life of Faith,” he imparted deep spiritual insight, touching the hearts of faculty members and students alike.

The message emphasized the importance of being rooted in Scripture and holding firm to God’s Word as the true foundation for faith, growth, and leadership in today’s world.

The event was part of a series of spiritual engagements aimed at nurturing the spiritual well-being of the UATC community while building strong global partnerships with mission-driven institutions.
