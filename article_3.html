UAUT/UATC Media Challenge 2025 – Registration is now open

June 13, 2025

The United African University of Tanzania (UAUT), in partnership with United African Technical College (UATC), invites aspiring journalists and storytellers to the Media Challenge 2025. The competition celebrates the upcoming launch of UATC’s Diploma in Journalism & Media Production and provides a platform for young talent to showcase skills in writing, presenting, filmmaking, and photography.

Who Can Participate

  • Form Four and Form Six students
  • Certificate holders in media/journalism or related fields
  • Anyone qualified and interested in pursuing media or journalism

Competition Categories

  • News Writing
  • Voice of the Future – News Anchor Challenge
  • Short Film Production
  • Creative Media Writing
  • Photojournalism

Theme

“The Past and Future of Tanzania’s Tourism Industry through the Eyes of Youth.”

Key Dates

  • Registration Opens: June 15, 2025
  • Registration Closes: August 15, 2025
  • Judging: August 16 – August 30, 2025
  • Results & Award Ceremony: September 10, 2025 (UAUT Campus)

Awards & Scholarships

  • 1st Place: 1,000,000 TZS + Scholarship
  • 2nd Place: 800,000 TZS + Scholarship
  • 3rd Place: 500,000 TZS + Scholarship

How to Apply

Submit your entry via the official registration form. You’ll provide personal details, education background, choose a category, and upload your work (plus a passport photo and supporting documents).
