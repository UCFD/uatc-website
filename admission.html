Entry Requirements

  • NTA Level 4 (Certificate): CSEE with ≥ 4 passes.
  • NTA Level 5 (Diploma Year 1): NTA 4 (related) or CSEE + bridging (where applicable).
  • NTA Level 6 (Diploma Year 2): NTA 5 (related).

Specific programs may set additional criteria. Contact Admissions for clarifications.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which programs are available?+
Certificate & Diploma programs at NTA 4–6 in CIT, BIT, Business Administration, Accounting & Finance, and Journalism & Media Production.
Can I pay tuition in installments?+
Yes. See each fee schedule above for installment notes (typically four equal installments).
Do I need to pay NHIF if I already have insurance?+
No. Attach a copy of your valid insurance card to your bank slip instead.
