STAR 2025 Wrap-Up: Startup Training for African Revival at UAUT

August 11, 2025

The 5-day 2025 Startup Training for African Revival (STAR) at UAUT has officially wrapped up — and what an incredible journey it has been!

From entrepreneurship insights to life-changing strategies, this international program brought together brilliant minds, powerful ideas, and unforgettable connections.

Highlights at a Glance

  • Hands-on startup design sprints and problem-solution fit
  • Mentorship on lean validation, pitching, and fundraising
  • Team formation across disciplines for real-world challenges
  • Showcase pitches with actionable feedback

A huge thank you to Handong Global University, all the facilitators, students, and media teams who made this event impactful and inspiring. The future of African innovation starts now!

What’s Next?

  • Follow-up mentorship and incubation pathways
  • Opportunities to pilot projects with partners
  • Community meetups and innovation challenges at UAUT/UATC
